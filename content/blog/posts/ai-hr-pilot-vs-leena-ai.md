## The Real Question: Are You an Enterprise or an SMB?

This decision is not about features. It is about organizational reality.

Enterprise HR operations have dedicated HR technology teams, established HRIS ecosystems (Workday, SAP, Oracle), global workforces, complex compliance requirements spanning multiple jurisdictions and procurement processes that can absorb a 6-month implementation timeline.

SMB HR operations typically have one HR generalist (or a founder wearing the HR hat), a lightweight HRIS (BambooHR, Gusto, Rippling), a U.S.-focused workforce, compliance anxiety without compliance expertise and zero tolerance for 6-month implementation projects.

If you are honest about which category your company falls into, the product choice becomes obvious.
